Common Questions

Does BizJetParts stock Falcon 50 parts, or source them on demand?

We don't hold physical inventory. We source parts on demand through our supplier and broker network, then quote pricing and lead time once a part is located and verified.

What engine does the Falcon 50 use?

The Falcon 50 is a tri-jet powered by three Honeywell (Garrett) TFE731-3-1C turbofan engines.

Does the Falcon 50 use a single FMS unit across all aircraft?

No. FMS fit varies by aircraft and retrofit history — most commonly Universal UNS-1C or UNS-1D. Confirm your installed variant before requesting a quote.
